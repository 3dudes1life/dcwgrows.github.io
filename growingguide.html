üçÖ Tomato Growing Guide

What You Need:
Full Sun, Well-draining soil, 5-gallon container, Support, Deep watering, Calcium-rich fertilizer

Best Planting Times:
South: Mar-Apr & Aug-Sep
West: Apr-May
Midwest: Late Apr-May
NE: May-Jun
PNW: May-Jun

How to Grow:
Plant Deep, Water Regularly, Feed Every 2-3 Weeks, Prune Suckers, Support Early

When to Harvest:
Fully colored, Slightly soft, Pick regularly

Pro Tip: Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before the last frost date.

What You Need:
Full Sun, Good Soil, 3-5 gallon pot, Even Moisture, Optional Support, Balanced Fertilizer

Best Planting Times:
South: Mar-Apr & Aug-Sep
West: Apr-May
Midwest: Late Apr-May
NE: May-Jun
PNW: May-Jun

How to Grow:
Start Indoors (Optional), Plant Deep, Water Evenly, Feed Every 2-3 Weeks, Provide Support, Watch for Pests

When to Harvest:
Green: Firm, Shiny, 3-4 inches

Pro Tip: Mulch to retain moisture and prevent weeds.
