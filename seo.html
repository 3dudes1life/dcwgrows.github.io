DCW Grows – California Plant Shop & Gardening Resource

DCW Grows is a California-based outdoor plant shop specializing in succulents, vegetables, herbs, and seasonal garden plants. We provide detailed growing guides, beginner-friendly gardening education, and plant care tips to help gardeners grow confidently.

What We Grow

  • Outdoor succulents
  • Tomatoes (heirloom & hybrid)
  • Bell peppers & hot peppers
  • Herbs including basil, thyme, rosemary & oregano
  • Watermelon, pumpkins & squash
  • Strawberries & berries
  • Brassicas including broccoli & cabbage
  • Leafy greens like kale, lettuce & spinach

Gardening Education

We believe gardening should be simple, sustainable, and magical. Our Garden Wizard helps answer common plant care questions including watering schedules, soil selection, planting times, pest control, and organic growing methods.

Who We Serve

DCW Grows serves beginner and intermediate gardeners looking to grow food at home in California and warm climates. Whether you're starting a backyard garden, raised bed garden, patio containers, or fruit trees — we help you grow successfully.
