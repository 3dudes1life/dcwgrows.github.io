These are the growing tips for the plants that are currently on sale on Etsy.

Calandrinia:

Take stem cuttings, plant in well-draining soil, water weekly, or let it self-seed.

Spiny Texas Prickly Pear:

Take a pad in spring, let it heal for a few days, then plant upright in drainage soil.

Santa Rita Prickly Pear:

Same as aboveâ€”cut, cure, then plant in well-draining soil.

Drosanthemum hispidum ("Purple Rosea Iceplant"):

Use cuttings, plant stem-down, water weekly till established, then reduce frequency.

Senecio mandraliscae ("Blue Chalk Sticks"):

Propagate from cuttings and water weekly, or allow seedlings to self-populate.

Christmas Fern:

Likes partial shade. Plant in moist, rich soil. Water regularly till established.

Vine (Solid Green):

Take 6-inch node cuttings, root in water, then transfer to soil. Keep lightly moist.

Vine (Variegated):

Take 6-inch node cuttings, root in water, then plant in soil. Thrives in indirect light.

Geranium:

Take stem cuttings, plant in well-draining soil, water weekly, and allow to dry between watering.
